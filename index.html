I'm a neuroscientist, budding bioinformatician and former Science Editor for The Ubyssey. I deeply admire people who take care of themselves & others, pay attention to & question media sources, and those who listen before speaking. For at least the next few months, I'll be looking for genes associated with neurodegeneration in RNAseq data. For the rest of my life, I'm hoping to continue examining neuroscientific data while continuing my writing. I was raised to understand the self as something, then nothing, then everything. I've found resonance for these most interesting of all ideas in Advaita Vedanta, scientific materialism, cross-cultural philosophy of mind, phenomenology, nondual spirituality, and fiction of all sorts.
